# AI Center of Excellence Design (MUST)

## Purpose

Design an AI enablement model that scales standards, support, and governance without becoming a delivery bottleneck.

## Trigger

Use when an organization needs a reusable structure for AI enablement across teams or business units.

## Rules (MUST)

- Define the center's responsibilities for standards, enablement, review, governance, and reusable assets.
- Separate central enablement duties from product-team ownership and accountability.
- Make intake, escalation, exception handling, and support boundaries explicit.
- Optimize for reusable leverage, not for centralizing all delivery work.
- Do not design a center of excellence as an approval bottleneck without service and accountability logic.

## Fallback

- If organizational scope or team model is unclear, keep the design provisional and name the unresolved boundary decisions.

## Minimal Output Contract

- center mandate and scope
- role split between center and delivery teams
- intake and escalation model
- reusable enablement assets
- unresolved boundary risks
