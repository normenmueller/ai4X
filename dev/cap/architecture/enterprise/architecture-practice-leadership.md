# Architecture Practice Leadership (MUST)

## Purpose

Create the strategic, governance, and sponsorship conditions for an effective architecture practice across the enterprise.

## Trigger

Use when architecture work needs executive alignment, practice leadership, or cross-domain mandate before downstream enterprise, segment, or solution work can succeed.

## Rules (MUST)

- Translate architecture significance into business and strategic change terms that senior stakeholders can act on.
- Keep architecture-practice mandate, governance, and change roadmaps aligned across the enterprise.
- Distinguish executive sponsorship and architecture-practice leadership from detailed segment or solution design ownership.
- Use leadership to create working conditions for downstream architects instead of substituting their responsibilities.

## Fallback

- If executive mandate, business drivers, or governance boundaries are unclear, keep the output provisional and name the missing sponsorship decisions.

## Minimal Output Contract

- strategic architecture concern
- executive alignment or sponsorship position
- practice or governance implication
- delegated next architecture action
