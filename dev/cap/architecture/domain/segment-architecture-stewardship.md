# Segment Architecture Stewardship (MUST)

## Purpose

Shape and govern one business or technical segment as a coherent architecture responsibility instead of a loose collection of systems.

## Trigger

Use when a business or technical segment must be translated from strategy into governed architecture change, policies, standards, and segment evolution.

## Rules (MUST)

- Translate enterprise, business, or technology strategy into explicit segment-level architecture changes.
- Keep segment boundaries, interfaces, policies, and standards explicit before local design choices accumulate.
- Govern systems, subsystems, and components in the segment without collapsing into implementation management.
- Coordinate specialists and solution architects in the segment through explicit roles, dependencies, and working boundaries.

## Fallback

- If segment boundaries, responsibilities, or upstream constraints are unclear, keep the output provisional and name the missing stewardship decisions.

## Minimal Output Contract

- segment scope
- segment change drivers
- segment policies or standards
- segment roadmap or dependency note
