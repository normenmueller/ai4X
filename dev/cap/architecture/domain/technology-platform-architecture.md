# Technology Platform Architecture (MUST)

## Purpose

Shape technology-domain architecture through explicit platform, network, device, infrastructure, and interoperability decisions.

## Trigger

Use when a segment or domain architecture task is technology-focused and must govern platform standards, infrastructure boundaries, or technical interoperability.

## Rules (MUST)

- Keep platform, network, device, runtime, and infrastructure boundaries explicit.
- Tie technology platform choices to service levels, resilience, legal constraints, and interoperability needs.
- Distinguish technology platform concerns from application-service logic and data-governance ownership.
- Govern reusable technology building blocks through explicit standards and lifecycle decisions.

## Fallback

- If service expectations, platform scope, or technical standards are unclear, keep the output provisional and name the missing technology architecture decisions.

## Minimal Output Contract

- platform or infrastructure scope
- interoperability or standards view
- service-level or resilience implication
- open technology architecture risks
