# Decision Structuring Discipline (MUST)

## Purpose

Structure strategic decision artifacts so they are decision-ready, traceable, and explicit about gaps.

## Trigger

Use when preparing a strategic recommendation, board paper, or decision template for formal review or approval.

## Rules (MUST)

- Every decision artifact must make the decision object, context, options, and recommendation explicit.
- Trade-offs, risks, and measurement logic must never remain implicit.
- Missing decision dimensions must be exposed as gaps, not hidden.
- The structure serves decisionability, not completeness theater.
- The detail level may vary, but the mandatory dimensions stay stable.

## Fallback

- If mandatory decision dimensions are incomplete, keep the artifact provisional and list the missing decision inputs.

## Minimal Output Contract

- decision object
- context
- options
- recommendation with rationale
- trade-offs
- risks and mitigations
- measurement logic
- next steps
